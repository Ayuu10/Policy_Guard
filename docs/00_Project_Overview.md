# PolicyGuard AI

## Vision

PolicyGuard AI is an AI-powered compliance platform that analyzes organizational documents and evaluates compliance against multiple regulatory frameworks using Machine Learning, Rule-Based Validation, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and Large Language Models.

The platform supports:

- GDPR
- HIPAA
- PCI DSS
- CCPA
- UK GDPR

Future support:

- ISO 27001
- SOC2
- NIST CSF
- EU AI Act
- LGPD

## Objectives

1. Analyze uploaded policies.
2. Detect missing or violated compliance requirements.
3. Explain findings.
4. Suggest fixes.
5. Rewrite problematic sections.
6. Generate professional reports.
7. Provide an AI compliance assistant.
